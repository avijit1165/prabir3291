District Governor, 2021-22

Prabir Chatterjee


Prabir joined Rotary in1999 and presently is a member of Rotary Calcutta Majestic. He is a Multiple Paul Harris Fellow.

Born in the famous CHATTERJEE family in Kolkata he did his Schooling from Mitra Institution, Bhowanipur and completed B.Sc from St. Xavier’s College, Kolkata followed by Diploma in Electronics & Video Technology.

A First generation Entrepreneur and Director of TEEVEETRONICS POLYTECHNIC PVT. LTD.

Prabir served Rotary District 3291 in different positions after completing his Presidentship in 2007-08. He served the District as Web Master twice, Secretary Events and Secretary Administration twice in 2013-14 & 2017-18 – a very rare feather in his cap.

He is a RLI faculty member since 2015. He is also the recipient of “Vocational Service Leadership Award” from Rotary International in 2014.

He is an avid Photographer and a passionate traveller. In 2016 he drove to Jaisalmeer, Rajasthan from Kolkata and covered 5,219 kms.

Spouse Rubena is a professional Dancer and currently the Senior School Co-curricular Coordinator at Sushila Birla Girls’ School, Kolkata. She is a Rotarian since 2012 and presently member of Rotary Calcutta Majestic.

Daughter Kathakali has completed her Masters in ‘Public Relations & Event Management’ from AMITY University, Noida.
